Cleaning Up Checkbox Columns Columns¶

There are 6 columns represent a checkbox question. The respondent was asked Which of the following Star Wars films have you seen? Please select all that apply., and then was able to check off a series of boxes indicating which movies they saw.

    "Star Wars: Episode IV  A New Hope": True,

def Most_seen(df):
    y = df[df.columns[3:9]].sum()
    ax = sns.barplot(x=df.columns[3:9], y=y)
    for p in ax.patches:
        ax.annotate(
            np.round(p.get_height(),decimals=2), 
            (p.get_x()+p.get_width()/2., p.get_height()), 
            ha='center', va='center', xytext=(0, 10), 
            textcoords='offset points')
    return plt.show()
